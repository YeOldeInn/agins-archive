Here are some quest packs to download. They are zipped files with text descriptions and bitmap file maps. If you have any questions or comments about any of the quests, email them to:
innkeep 'at-sign' aginsinn.com.

I am always interested in your quests. If you have any that you have written, I will post them here for you. You will receive full credit for your work. You can use your own format, or download the icons that I use below. I can even convert your old paper quests to electronic format if you wish. If you have any quests at all, please let me know!

Some of these quests need additional figures to play. Don't let it keep you from downloading them if you don't have the figures. Many contain conversions to use regular Heroquest monsters, and if they don't you can always convert them yourself.

Check out the Tile Printing Page for some tips on printing tiles.
