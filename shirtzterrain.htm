The Temple comes from the old Mattel Fantasy board game, Dark World. Extra bits were added to the Temple posts using various GW bitz. The Temple was glued to a hardboard base, with pieces of blue insulation board broken up and attached to simulate boulders. Watered down glue was brushed on and three types of model terrain dirt and stone were sprinkled throughout. Everything then was primed black, then progressively drybrushed with Shadow gray, Codex grey, Reaper Stone grey, Bleached bone, and a final, careful drybrushing of white. The temple was given a wash of Rustal to give it a motte pattern and to "warm up" the otherwise cold grey colors. Deer moss/lichen was superglue in various cracks in the rocks.
