Can't afford new miniatures, but want to enhance your game with new monsters and heroes?
Then you should consider paper miniatures. Download them and print off as
many as you need!

Note that while I have given the figures names (for example the first set is named Evil Wizards), you don't have to use the minis as named. Feel free to use them in any way you wish!

Steps to use minis:
  1. Cut the mini out
  2. Fold in half
  3. Tape or glue one of the bottom tabs onto the other
You can purchase many of these minis pre-printed on cardstock from Perilous Quests
