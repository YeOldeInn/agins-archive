The Palace Garden

The real politics of the Empire are not determined in the throne room or in parliament,
but in the renown Palace garden. Strolling along the manicured trees and perfumed flowers,
with conversation masked by the running water of the fountains, kings, prime ministers, and
and diplomats negotate treaties that will decide the course of many kingdoms.

The garden has also been the scene of the occasional assassination of royal and elected officials.
It has also served as a discreet meeting place for secret lovers. Here's an opportunity to have your characters
use thier charisma and social skills instead of swordplay and spells!
A refreshing change of location for your party of adventurers
who are weary of one too many trips into slimy dungeons and dusty catacombs!

Dimension One

There are countless alternative dimensions that exist in the magical cosmic plane.
Is this one a prison of some wizard, held captive for several millennia?
Does that endless running stream of water hold any healing properties?
Perhaps it is an illusion to disorient a group of adventurers?

Blue Stone Temple

Built by a forgotten civilization, this temple laid hidden for many centuries.
What's the secret of it's huge blue polished stone?
And who (or what!) has been keeping the temple maintained
and cleared from the vegetation of the encroaching forest?
