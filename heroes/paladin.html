THE PALADIN

by Andy Sekela

You are the Paladin, a Holy Knight on a mission to rid the world of the evil of Zargon. You may use any Weapon (except a Crossbow), and wear any Armor. You may also use a Holy Symbol. You are immune to all Chaos Spells. Once per Quest, you may Heal yourself or an adjacent Hero (up to 2 lost Body Points).

Statistics:
Attack Dice: 2
Defend Dice: 3
Body Points: 7
Mind Points: 3
Movement: 2 Red Dice
Starting Weapon: Shortsword
Starting Armor: Shield
