THE SHAPESHIFTER

by Andy Sekela

You are the Shapeshifter, able to take the form of a number of different animals. Even you do not know how you got this power. You may never use any Weapon or wear any Armor. Instead, you rely on your natural animal instincts. You use the attack and defense dice of your chosen form. You may change form at the beginning of any turn.

Statistics:
Attack Dice: SPECIAL
Defend Dice: SPECIAL
Body Points: 7
Mind Points: 3
Movement: SPECIAL
Starting Weapons: None
Starting Armor: None


Shapeshifter Forms
  • Giant Eagle
    As the Eagle, you may fly over any occupied square, although you may not end your turn in one. You may also fly over trap squares without springing the trap. You may not open doors.

    Statistics for Eagle:
    Attack Dice 2
    Defense Dice 2
    Movement 2 Red Dice

  • Grizzly Bear
    As the Bear, you may open doors (by breaking them down), but may not climb out of Pits. If you hit with all 4 Attack Dice you automatically kill your opponent.

    Statistics for Bear:
    Attack Dice 4
    Defense Dice 3
    Movement 1 Red Die

  • Tiger
    As the Tiger, if you spring a Trap roll 1 die. If you roll any shield you take no damage though the Trap is sprung. You automatically jump over Pits. You may not open doors, except Secret Doors.

    Statistics for Tiger:
    Attack Dice 3
    Defense Dice 4
    Movement 3 Red Dice
