THE HALFLING THIEF

by Andy Sekela

You are a small but brave warrior. You may only use a Dagger, Shortsword, and Shortbow, and may only wear Leather Armor. You can disarm traps without a Tool Kit. You may draw 2 Treasure Cards instead of 1, and discard 1 of them. You may search for Treasure or Traps even when you can see a monster.

Statistics:
Attack Dice: 2
Defend Dice: 2
Body Points: 5
Mind Points: 4
Movement: 2 Red Dice
Starting Weapon: Dagger
Starting Armor: None
