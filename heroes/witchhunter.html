THE WITCH HUNTER

by Andy Sekela

You are the Witch Hunter. You are an expert at destroying suprenatural foes, and use an arsenal of special weapons to do battle. You may choose any 3 of the 4 equipment cards to take with you on a Quest. You may use any Amor or Weapon, including a Holy Symbol.

Statistics:
Attack Dice: 2
Defend Dice: 2
Body Points: 6
Mind Points: 5
Movement: 2 Red Dice
Starting Weapon: Shortsword
Starting Armor: None
