THE MYSTIC

by Andy Sekela

You are a Mystic, a member of a highly secret society. You may only use a Staff, and may never wear any Armor. You may attack any Monster (except Undead) up to 3 squares away with a Psychic Strike. Roll 1 die for every 2 Mind Points you have (round up). The Monster defends with 1 die for each of its Mind Points. You may also choose 2 of the 4 Psychic Powers.

Statistics:
Attack Dice: 2
Defend Dice: 2
Body Points: 4
Mind Points: 6
Movement: 2 Red Dice
Starting Weapon: Staff
Starting Armor: None
