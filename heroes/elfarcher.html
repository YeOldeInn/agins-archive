ELF ARCHER

by Andy Sekela

You are the Elf Archer. Renowned among your kind for your master with the bow, you use your skills to defeat evil whenever you can. You may not use a Battle Axe or Shield or wear Plate Mail. You can shoot your bow at any non-adjacent monster. You may even shoot through occupied squares (although you roll one less attack die).

Statistics:
Attack Dice: 2 with Shortsword
3 with Bow
Defend Dice: 2
Body Points: 6
Mind Points: 4
Movement: 2 Red Dice
Starting Weapons: Shortsword and Bow
Starting Armor: None
