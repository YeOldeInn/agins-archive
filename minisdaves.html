Minis By Dave Wile


The dungeon setting is the Master Maze from Dwarven Forge. To use it with Hero Quest is simplicity itself.
First I "build" the dungeon using the set....then I use small felt quares to cover the dungeon piece by piece.
As the Heroes move to new sections, I simply remove the felt squares to revel what they "see."
Loads of fun.
