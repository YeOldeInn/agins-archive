Terrible Gargoyle



Stronger and more skilled in weapons than their normal Gargoyle brethren.

Stats:
Movement: 6 Squares
Attack Dice: 4 With Sword
2 With Whip
Defense Dice: 5
Body Points: 3
Mind Points: 4


Terrible Gargoyle Monster Card US Cardback
Terrible Gargoyle Monster Card European Cardback


Attacks with both weapons on same turn, but must attack the same Hero with both weapons. May attack Diagonally with both weapons.

You can use the Gargoyle mini for the Terrible Gargoyle. If you have more than one Gargoyle mini, when using the Terrible Gargoyle with regular Gargoyles, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
