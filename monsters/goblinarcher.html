Goblin Archer



These Goblins have bows which they use to shoot at their enemies from a distance.

Stats:
Movement: 10 Squares
Attack Dice: 2
Defense Dice: 1
Body Points: 1
Mind Points: 1




Goblin Archers can shoot their arrows at any Hero in their line-of-sight. Use the same rules as a Hero with a bow.

Goblin Archer Monster Card US Cardback
Goblin Archer Monster Card European Cardback


You can use Goblin minis for Goblin Archers. When using them with regular Goblins, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc). Another idea is to use only Goblins with a certain type of weapons as Goblin Archers.
