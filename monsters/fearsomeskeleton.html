Fearsome Skeleton



These horrible Undead creatures inflict horror and fear into the very soul of their adversaries!

Stats:
Movement: 6 Squares
Attack Dice: 2
Defense Dice: 2
Body Points: 1
Mind Points: 0

Fearsome Monsters:
Anytime a Hero starts his turn in the same room as a Fearsome Monster, he must make a 'Fear' roll at the start of his turn. The Hero rolls 1 red die, and on a roll of 5-6 he has become afraid and must move as far as possible from the monster, even if it means leaving the room. The Hero may not make any attack that turn, even against another monster, but can defend. If a Hero enters a room with a Fearsome Monster during his movement turn, he must make a Fear roll immediately. The fear only lasts one turn, but the Hero must make a fear roll every turn he is in the same room as a Fearsome Monster. Once a Hero has passed a fear roll, he does not have to make it again that turn, but must on the next if he is still in contact with a Fearsome Monster.

You can use Skeleton minis for Fearsome Skeletons. When using them with regular Skeletons, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
