Shapeshifter
by The Shapeshifter (1K)



Use the Chaos Warlord figure
M
A
D
B
M
8* 2* 3* 3* 5

Comments:
  • Can cast the following spells: Tempest and Cloud of Chaos
  • Can Shape Shift
    Select any number of monster cards and shuffle them.
    When the shapeshifter reaches 0 body points you draw one
    monster card from the deck. The shapeshifter turns into this
    monster.

    *The shapeshifter takes on all stats except mind points of
    the new monster. Return the card to the deck and resuffle.
    This process continues until
    You draw a monster card that has already been drawn, the shapeshifter is dead
Options:
The shapeshifter could start out as a diffrent monster besides the chaos warlord, such as a mummy king or an orc shaman.

Change the number of monster cards in your deck to change the difficulty

Use only certain types of monsters in your deck. If the shapeshifter
is a mummy king then use a deck of undead monsters.

Change spells to make shapeshifter easier, harder, or to make it fit
its form. (If mummy king give is summon undead)

Optional ways to kill shapeshifter
option b) You draw the same monster card twice in a row, the
shapeshifter is dead (Can be very difficult)

option c) The shapeshifter can only change form one per round.
Example: The barbarian brings the shapeshifter to 0 body points so it
changes into an orc. Then the dwarf rolls good enough to kill the
orc. Since the shapeshifter has already changed this round it is
dead. (A round is once through everyones turn.)
