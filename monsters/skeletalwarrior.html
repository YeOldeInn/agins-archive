Skeletal Warrior



The minions of Chaos search the world for the bodies of mighty warriors slain in combat. These are raised to life as Skeletal Warriors to do battle with the Heroes.

Stats:
Movement: 6 Squares
Attack Dice: 3
Defense Dice: 2
Body Points: 2
Mind Points: 0


Skeletal Warrior Monster Card US Cardbacks
Skeletal Warrior Monster Card European Cardback


You can use Skeleton minis for Skeletal Warriors. When using them with regular Skeletons, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
