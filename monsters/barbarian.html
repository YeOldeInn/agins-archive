Barbarian



Have an extra Barbarian figure? Why not use him as a monster? Barbarians are able fighters, preferring a two-handed sword. They disdain armor, wearing only fur (even during the warmest of summers).

Stats:
Movement: 9 Squares
Attack Dice: 4
Defense Dice: 2
Body Points: 6
Mind Points: 2


Barbarian Monster Card US Cardback
Barbarian Monster Card European Cardback


Barbarians may attack diagonally.

You can use the Barbarian mini for the Barbarian. If you have more than one Barbarian mini, when using the Barbarian as a monster while a Hero is playing the Barbarian you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
