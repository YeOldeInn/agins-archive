Diseased Zombie



These Zombies are carriers of dread diseases and plagues. Anytime they make a successful hit against a Hero, they may pass the the infection to him!

Stats:
Movement: 5 Squares
Attack Dice: 2
Defense Dice: 3
Body Points: 1
Mind Points: 0


Diseased Zombie Monster Card US Cardback
Diseased Zombie Monster Card European Cardback


Diseased:
A Hero who is hit by a diseased monster will lose the normal body points from the dice roll. This Hero must then roll one red die. On a roll of 1 or 2, the Hero is diseased. On the Hero's next turn he will lose a Body Point from the plague, plus another Body Point every second turn thereafter. If you are playing with 'Potion of Cure' potions from the Potion Shop, than this is the only potion that cures Diseases (Healing potions allow the Hero to regain Body Points, but not cure the disease). If you are not playing with 'Potions of Cure', then a Healing Potion or spell cures the disease.

You can use Zombie minis for Diseased Zombies. When using them with regular Zombies, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
