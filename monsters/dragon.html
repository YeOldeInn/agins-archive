Three Legged Dragon
by Azrael


Download the Three Legged Dragon Monster Card (148K)

  • This is an unusual dragon as it only has 3 legs this not only impairs movement but also increases the number of attacks by 1
  • This is the same dragon as depicted in Ron Shirtz' excellent tile dragon under ice.
  • Beware he is very very hard to kill
  • I use the dragon in a way that the heroes release it on an earlier quest
  • A famous knight is then despatched to slay the dragon He only succeeds in lopping off a leg before being devoured.
  • It is now up to the heroes to put right the wrongs there actions have caused
  • If you want to use a 4 legged dragon give it an additional attack dice roll of 3 dice
rules
  • The dragon has 1 claw attack of 3 dice, 1 tail attack of 3 dice and 1 fireball breath attack of 10 dice
  • The claw attack affects all adjacent squares to the front of the dragon
  • the tail attack affects all squares 2 tiles away from the rear of the dragon
  • The fireball attack affects an entire room or corridor,all craetures apart from the dragon must defend, any treasure in the affected area is destroyed
  • all closed doors are classed as opened
  • The dragon is immune to all missile weapons apart from magical missiles
  • The dragon is immune to all fire based spells and creatures
  • The dragon has all fire spells to be used once per quest
The model is from Grenadier miniatures
Most model Manufacturers have Dragons in their ranges

This monster can be used in both UK and US versions
