Balrog



A Balrog is every Hero's nightmare. Strong, tough, an excellent fighter, and has the ability to cast magical spells!

Stats:
Movement: 6 Squares
Attack Dice: 4 With Sword
2 With Whip
Defense Dice: 5
Body Points: 5
Mind Points: 4


Balrog Monster Card US Cardback
Balrog Monster Card European Cardback


Attacks with both weapons on same turn, but must attack the same Hero with both weapons. May attack Diagonally with both weapons. May also cast the Chaos Spell Firestorm three times.

You can use the Gargoyle mini for the Balrog. If you have more than one Gargoyle mini, when using the Balrog with regular Gargoyles, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
