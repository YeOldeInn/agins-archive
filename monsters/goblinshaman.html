Goblin Shaman
by Azrael



Download the Goblin Shaman Monster Card (124K)

  • The shaman makes a good character monster but can be easily killed by heroes so ensure he is always protected by bodyguards i.e. orcs/orc biguns/trolls
  • He has access to all six orc shaman cards but this can be modified to suit the quest being undertaken
The model is from Warhammer Quest - the goblin spearman has been modified with the addition of a skull from the game to make his spear look like a staff
Most model Manufacturers have goblin shaman in their ranges

This monster can be used in both UK and US versions
