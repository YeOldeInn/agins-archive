Ghoul



Related to Zombies, these Undead creatures are less decayed and more dangerous. Ghouls prefer to hunt in packs, attacking unmercifully to bring their victims down.

Stats:
Movement: 5 Squares
Attack Dice: 3
Defense Dice: 3
Body Points: 1
Mind Points: 0


Ghoul Monster Card US Cardback
Ghoul Monster Card European Cardback


You can use Zombie minis for Ghouls. When using them with regular Zombies, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
