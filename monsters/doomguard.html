DoomGuard



Morcar/Zargon's Elite guard, the DoomGuard is a special squad of Chaos Warriors that protects the Evil Wizard.

Stats:
Movement: 7 Squares
Attack Dice: 4
Defense Dice: 5
Body Points: 3
Mind Points: 3


DoomGuard Monster Card US Cardback
DoomGuard Monster Card European Cardback


DoomGuards only need to roll one black shield to block all hits made by a single attacker.


You can use Chaos Warrior minis for DoomGuards. When using them with regular Chaos Warriors, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
