Pharaoh



The Mummified remains of ancient kings, Pharaohs retain much of their regal training in the art of battle.

Stats:
Movement: 4 Squares
Attack Dice: 4
Defense Dice: 4
Body Points: 2
Mind Points: 0


Pharaoh Monster Card US Cardback
Pharaoh Monster Card European Cardback


You can use Mummy minis for Pharaohs. When using them with regular Mummies, you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
