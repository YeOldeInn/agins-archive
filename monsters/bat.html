Bat
by Mark Miller



Download the Bat Monster Card (187K)

This monster is for the U.S. game system. It figure came out of a Games Workshop box. it says FT01 on the front if that helps. The batch number on the back was 758500 for this box. Product code: 99 12 02 99 003. Component Code: 99 28 02 99 001.

Special rules/ explination:
The Bat has a high movement (10) because it is small and can fly (a faster means of traveling through the dungeons). It has a low attack (1) because it is small and weak, plus taking into consideration its special abilities. It defends at (2) just because it seemed to play the best that way. (1) body point is a standard as poison kills you quickly (want to keep it fair). The mind point rests at (1) because the bats are not intelligent.

Can fly over any figure when moving: This ability allows the Bat to move over any Hero or Monster as long as it has enough movement left, and there is a free space.
