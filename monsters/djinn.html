Djinn
by Jason Kibble



Download the Djinn Monster Card (84K)



Most fantasy lines carry some sort of djinn or genie figure.
Ral Partha #01-069 no link
Reaper Minis #1212 http://www.reapermini.com/DDFrame.htm

With a somewhat dark sense of humor and unlimited movement, the Djinn can be a nasty enemy. While he can cast any chaos spell, often he prefers 'Command' so the heroes must fight one of their own. The Djinn never attacks if there are other monsters in the same room/corridor, however, he will attack if he is alone. A Djinn cannot be killed - when he reaches 0 Body Points, he will automatically grant the three wishes found below, cast 'Cloud of Chaos' (even if he has already cast it before) and disappear in a puff of smoke.

Three wishes
Wealth: All heroes divide 500 gold coins
Power: Every spellcaster may choose one spell already spent and return it to their hand
Health: All heroes roll one red die. Each hero gains body points equal to the number rolled (not to exceed starting level)
