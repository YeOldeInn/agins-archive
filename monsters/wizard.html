Wizard



Have an extra Wizard figure? Why not use him as a monster? Wizards have little in the way of fighting skills, prefering to use their great intellect and magical abilities to fight battles.

Stats:
Movement: 6 Squares
Attack Dice: 1
Defense Dice: 2
Body Points: 3
Mind Points: 6


Wizard Monster Card US Cardback
Wizard Monster Card European Cardback


Wizards may attack diagonally. They may also cast up to NINE spells (GameMaster's Choice). They can be either Hero or Chaos spells.

You can use the Wizard mini for the Wizard. If you have more than one Wizard mini, when using the Wizard as a monster while a Hero is playing the Wizard you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
