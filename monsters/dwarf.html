Dwarf



Have an extra Dwarf figure? Why not use him as a monster? Dwarves are sturdy fighters, preferring axes, hammers, and Dwarvish armor.

Stats:
Movement: 5 Squares
Attack Dice: 2
Defense Dice: 5
Body Points: 5
Mind Points: 3


Dwarf Monster Card US Cardback
Dwarf Monster Card European Cardback


You can use the Dwarf mini for the Dwarf. If you have more than one Dwarf mini, when using the Dwarf as a monster while a Hero is playing the Dwarf you should make them different in some way (paint, a cloth cape, a string for a belt, etc).
