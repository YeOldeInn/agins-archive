Cave Tiles



These tiles can be used to create your own cave scenes. They may be printed off and layed together in various ways to create new caves and dungeons. The Passages file has some ideas for rules to use when playing with these tiles.


You may want to use these tiles with the Cave Entrance tile, found on the Outdoor Tiles Page.
