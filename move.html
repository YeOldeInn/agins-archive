Move News



After three years of being on the free webservers, we've finally purchased our own space. It's been a rough three years. We outgrew Geocities in just a few months. First we moved to Xoom. This was fine for almost two years; then NBCI bought out Xoom, and started putting bandwidth restrictions on their sites. Because of the size and popularity of the site downloads, especially the tiles, we were pegged as a warez site and closed down (twice). So we moved to Fortunecity, Topcities, and finally 9ug. All of which decided to switch their service after we moved their. In the case of Topcities we didn't even get a chance to finish uploading the files before they changed.

Finally I got tired of the constant moving, and begun looking for a permanent site to move the webpage. Along with the move I purchased a domain name (Ron suggested the name AginsInn.com) so that if there is ever the need to move again I can keep the same address.

So what does this mean to you (besides reading a long winded sob-story)? Hopefully the biggest change will be faster access times for page loads and downloads. The free webservers were often VERY slow. Also once all the moving is finished I can begin regular updates again.

We ask you to please be patient during the move. I am going to update this page as I complete the moving of main pages (the buttons off the homepage). Please do not send any emails telling me that a link is broken if it does not appear below. The link is broken because I haven't moved it yet! :-) If you find a broken link in any of the pages below, please email me and let me know.
