NEW HEREO CONTEST



The November contest will be to create a new Hero. Here is the critera for the contest:

  1. Heroes should be in the basic format found in the Heroquest game. You may include any experience or training rules for your Hero if you desire, but only the basic format will be judged.
  2. Entries may be in either graphic, Hero card or in text versions. Grahpics will probably influence the judges more...
  3. You may include new spell cards, artifact cards, and so on if required.
  4. Entries should be received no later than 8:00 a.m. Central Time (US) on December 3, 2001. Entries received after this time will not be considered.
  5. All entries will be posted to the website, on both this page and on the New Heroes page.
  6. Mail all entries to Dewayne at arkcaver@yahoo.com .

Entries will be judged on playability, originality, and presentation. Good luck!
