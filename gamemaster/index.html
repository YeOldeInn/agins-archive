Whether you want to play HeroQuest

Solo or In a group without an Evil Wizard 

the HeroQuest Game Master is for you.
It removes the need for an Evil Wizard player, without taking anything away from your board game experience i.e. you still get to use all the figures, furniture, cards etc. and you still get to roll dice.

It is NOT an arcade game though - it's a utility.

Features

Game board revealed as you move along.

Chaos spells controlled/played by computer.

Traps - unseen until you detect them or activate them.
Trap damage variable (set at time of creation).

Monster icons.

Beastiary with monster stats pops up when a monster is encountered

Secret doors

Teleports

Overlay Tiles

Gamenotes activated when the map is uncovered

Field triggered messages

Load/Save game

Full quest creator with point and click interface

Monster A.I.

and lots more....

Enjoy!
