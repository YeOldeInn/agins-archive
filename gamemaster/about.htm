About

Aginsinn.com
 The HeroQuest Game Master was developed as an answer to my own playing groups problem - How to let everyone control a player. As things go, the utility matured into something that others could use too.

The excel version (released 8/2001) was working but lacking in ease of use and with new releases of excel something would always become incompatible. Therefore the standalone version was developed (jan/2002).

Thanks to Dewayne Agin & Ron Shirtz for letting this site reside on their server.

Steve Baloban was also very helpful in giving me some material I was missing.

If you have any suggestions, criticism, praise - whatever. don't hesitate to drop me a line.

In the meantime - Enjoy your gaming sessions
