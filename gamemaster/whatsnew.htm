20-09-02 Finally an update! The Frozen Horror quest pack and version 2.2 of HQGM is out.

"Dialogue" - you can now add NPC conversations with the Heroes.
"Add object" - You can add replace objects during the game. Good for surprises.
"Conditional trigger" Only if all heroes are in the room or the last hero has passed a square will the trap/event be triggered.

All treasure cards are implemented too. You choose what expansions are included in the treasure deck draws.
