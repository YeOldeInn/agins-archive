Quest pack installation

Each quest pack should be extracted WITH directories to "yourHQGMpath\quests\". 

General notes

The US version quest packs contain these differences from the EU version:

- Monsters have often more than one body point.
- Monster spell casters use Chaos spells not available to players.
- Maps , storyline, traps, monster types and monster numbers are almost identical.

US version quests are generally more challenging because of the additional body points.

If you have the EU version of the game you DO NOT NEED anything additional to play these quests (beyond what is described in the individual quest notes).

Against the Ogre Horde

As the name implies you get to fight Ogre's and lots of them! Morcar/Zargon is using an Ogre Clan to wreak havoc on the Emperor's lands. You must stop this.
This quest pack should be played after "Return of the Witch Lord" and "Kellar's Keep".

The quest pack used to non-standard play rules. 
Ogre's had variable body points and all Ogre's were linked in a quest. I've changed that to a more standard approach.
Secondly Chaos spellcasting is done with "Tokens" instead of normal spells. That too is changed to conform with the other quest packs.

Finally since this is an EU version it is VERY tough to play it with US monster stats. If you wan't to play it as intended, give all monsters except Ogres and special characters 1 body point.
